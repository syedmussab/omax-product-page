What Is Omax VoiceThru AI Order?

AI Voice Thru Order is an advanced, voice-driven ordering solution that lets customers place their orders - without clicks, queues, or human assistance.

Using state-of-the-art AI speech recognition and natural language understanding, it captures every detail with speed and precision, delivering a fast, seamless, and contactless ordering experience.

Built to modernize customer interactions, AI Voice Thru Order is ideal for businesses that want to automate ordering, reduce operational load, and offer a next-generation experience.

How It Works

Customer Speaks the Order

The customer simply speaks their meal order in English or Urdu through the drive-thru microphone or kiosk device or mobile device. No clicks just natural, hands-free interaction..

AI Understands & Processes the Request

Our AI instantly interprets the spoken order, identifying menu items, quantities, and customizations with high accuracy.

AI Confirms the Order

The system repeats the order back to the customer for confirmation, ensuring clarity and zero misunderstandings..

Order Sent to Your POS System

After confirmation, the final order is automatically delivered to your POS, kitchen display system, or whichever operational flow you use including payment management.

Customer Moves to Checkout

The customer proceeds to payment and pickup with a faster, smoother experience and no delays.
